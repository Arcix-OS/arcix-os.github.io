How does Arcix protect student privacy?
Arcix collects minimal telemetry; Arcix Mind runs locally by default and any cloud sync is governed by explicit consent and transparent funding reports.
Can I run Arcix on older hardware?
Yes — Arcix is optimized for low footprint deployments with lazy-loading services and optional light profiles for legacy devices.
How are contributors paid?
Transparent grant pools, bounties, and sponsored pilot funding pay contributors; we publish reports and payments publicly.
