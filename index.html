What makes Arcix special

A modern OS that blends desktop power with mobile clarity — privacy-first, lightweight, and community governed.

Unified Workspace

Floating windows, workspace templates, and task-focused layouts that adapt with you. Fast context switching, focused workflows.

Arcix Mind

On-device assistance that summarizes notes, suggests workflows, and automates repetitive tasks — offline-first with privacy controls.

Education-Ready

Teacher dashboards, offline content packs, and managed pilot tooling so schools can deploy without heavy infrastructure.

Community Funding

Transparent funding that pays contributors, funds grants, and keeps maintenance healthy over the long term.

Compatibility

Run Android apps, PWAs, and optional containers for advanced desktop workflows — bridging app ecosystems safely.

Security & Privacy

Per-app permissions, encrypted vaults, verified boot, and the minimum telemetry required to keep things secure.

Design & Technology Vision

Arcix brings a calm, consistent design language to system UI while enabling power users — a bridge between safety, privacy, and creativity.

Design system

Tokens, accessible components, and UX patterns that scale across devices.

Extensibility

Modular services, an approachable SDK, and clear platform APIs for third-party developers.

Low footprint

Optimised services, lazy-loaded components, and careful resource scheduling for older hardware.
