What makes Arcix special
A cinematic OS that balances beginner clarity with expert power — privacy by design, classroom-ready, and community-run. We're in active development; features will evolve with testing and community feedback.
Arcix Mind (optional)
On-device summarization & workflow help. Cloud features are explicit opt-in only.

Unified Workspace

Context-aware layouts, floating windows, and persistent workspaces across devices.

Arcix Connect

Wi-Fi-first calls & messaging with encrypted handoff between your devices (in development & pilot testing).

Education Tools

Teacher dashboards, offline bundles, and pilot tooling designed for low-cost deployments.

Compatibility

Android apps, PWAs, and containers for more advanced desktop apps — working toward wide compatibility.

Community Funding

50% of funds go to charity; 50% to maintenance & infrastructure. We publish funding reports for transparency.

Security & Privacy

Per-app permissions, verified boot, encrypted vaults, and strictly minimal telemetry.
