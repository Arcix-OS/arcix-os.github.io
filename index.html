Built for everyone — privacy-first & community-funded

A next-generation open-source operating system that unifies mobile and desktop computing. Designed for schools, creators, developers, and everyday people — Arcix is privacy-first, productivity-focused, and built so contributors are paid fairly.

Why Arcix OS?

Productivity, privacy, and purpose — in one open OS.

Unified Workspace

A single, adaptable workspace across phone, tablet, and PC — floating windows and task-focused layouts.

Arcix Mind

On-device assistance that summarizes notes, suggests workflows, and automates repetitive tasks — all offline by default.

Arcix Connect

High-quality calls and messaging over any WiFi network without cellular coverage. End-to-end encrypted with seamless device switching.

Community Funding

Transparent funding that pays contributors, funds education pilots, and supports long-term maintenance.

Security & Privacy

Per-app permissions, encrypted vaults, verified boot, and minimal telemetry by design.

App Compatibility

Android apps, PWAs, and optional containers for advanced desktop-style applications.

Cross-Device Communication

Start calls on your phone and continue on desktop. Integrated voicemail and messaging hub with offline message queuing.

WiFi-First Network

Make calls and send messages over any WiFi network without cellular coverage — perfect for schools and community spaces.

The Vision

Arcix OS aims to combine the clarity of mobile platforms with the power of desktop computing — all while remaining open, community-oriented, and accessible to everyone.

0.0 — Blueprint

Planning, brand design, and team formation.

0.1 — Ignite

Sync AOSP, prototype x86 desktop support.

0.5 — Evolve

Arcix Mind, classroom tools, and community pilots.

1.0 — Ascend

Stable desktop mode, education-ready builds, partner programs.
